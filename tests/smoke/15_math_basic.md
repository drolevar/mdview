# Math basics

Inline math: $E = mc^2$ and $\pi r^2$ work mid-sentence.

A fraction: $\frac{a + b}{c - d}$. Greek: $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$.

Display math:

$$
\int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

A matrix in display mode:

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}
$$

A piecewise definition:

$$
f(x) = \begin{cases}
  x^2 & x \ge 0 \\
  -x  & x < 0
\end{cases}
$$

Inline math should not trigger on $5 today and $7 tomorrow,
because the dollars are followed by digits.
